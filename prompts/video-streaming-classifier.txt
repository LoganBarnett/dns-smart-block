Your task:

DEFINITION OF "VIDEO STREAMING SITE"

A site SHOULD be classified as a video streaming site ("is_matching_site": true) if its
PRIMARY purpose is one or more of:

1. Being a video streaming platform:
   - Hosts and streams video content (movies, TV shows, user-generated videos).
   - Examples that SHOULD be TRUE:
     - youtube.com (video streaming platform)
     - netflix.com (subscription streaming service)
     - hulu.com (subscription streaming service)
     - twitch.tv (live streaming platform)
     - vimeo.com (video hosting and streaming)
     - dailymotion.com (video sharing platform)
     - crunchyroll.com (anime streaming)
     - disneyplus.com (Disney subscription streaming)
     - peacocktv.com (NBC streaming service)
     - paramountplus.com (Paramount streaming service)

2. Being a live streaming platform primarily for video content:
   - Live video broadcasts, whether gaming, entertainment, or other content.
   - Examples that SHOULD be TRUE:
     - kick.com (live streaming platform)
     - rumble.com (video streaming platform)

3. Being a short-form video platform:
   - Platforms dedicated to short video content.
   - Examples that SHOULD be TRUE:
     - tiktok.com (short-form video platform)
     - vine.co (short-form video, now defunct)

A site SHOULD NOT be classified as a video streaming site ("is_matching_site": false) if:

1. It is a general-purpose social network that happens to support video, but video
   is not its PRIMARY focus:
   - Examples that SHOULD be FALSE:
     - facebook.com (general social network with video features)
     - instagram.com (photo/video sharing, but not primarily streaming)
     - reddit.com (general forum platform with video support)
     - twitter.com/x.com (microblogging with video support)

2. It is a video conferencing or video chat platform (not streaming):
   - Examples that SHOULD be FALSE:
     - zoom.us (video conferencing)
     - meet.google.com (video conferencing)
     - whereby.com (video calls)
     - discord.com (chat platform with video calls)

3. It is about video technology, production, or equipment rather than streaming:
   - News about video streaming, production companies, etc.

4. It is a general-purpose content hosting platform where video is incidental:
   - Cloud storage, file hosting, etc.

5. The provided information is too vague or generic to clearly indicate
   a primary video streaming focus. In that case, be conservative and answer false
   with a lower confidence.

INPUT FORMAT

You will receive a single JSON object describing the site. It will always include:
- "domain": the domain name as a string.
- At least one free-text field with descriptive text, for example:
  - "summary", "description", "title", "meta_description", "og_description", etc.

The JSON may include MORE fields in the future. Treat all string fields as possible
clues about the site's purpose. Ignore fields you do not understand; do NOT fail
because of unknown fields.

IMPORTANT OUTPUT RULES

- Output MUST be valid JSON.
- Output MUST have exactly these three fields at the top level:
  - "is_matching_site": boolean
  - "confidence": number between 0 and 1
  - "reasoning": string (1-2 sentences explaining your decision)
- Do NOT include any extra text, comments, or explanations outside the JSON.
- Do NOT wrap the JSON in backticks.
